Publications

Iris Ma, Cristina V. Lopes
May, 2023 In ICSE SEENG 23
Improving the Quality of Commit Messages in Students’ Projects

The research focuses on enhancing the quality of commit messages in collaborative software development by introducing specific requirements in the “what” and “why” parts through modifications to the GitHub Desktop application, demonstrating improved informativeness, clearness, and length in commit messages among students.
